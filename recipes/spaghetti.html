Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta. It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Pasta Noodles
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until tender.
  2. Cook ground beef until brown and season.
  3. Chop and cook onions.
  4. Add all ingredients to pot with sauce.
  5. Serve.
